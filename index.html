Year Established

Second Chance Aspin Shelter Philippines Inc.

Established in 2018, Second Chance Aspin Shelter Philippines Incorporated provided a home for strays, abused, and abandoned cats and dogs. Aspin or "Asong Pinoy" are Philippine native dogs, while Puspins ("Pusang Pinoy") are Philippine native cats. We strive to give them a second chance by preparing them for their furrever homes. Unfortunately, strays are rampant in the Philippines, and abandonment and cruelty are common. The shelter is located in Calamba, Laguna. Originally, SECASPI sheltered 40+ dogs and 20+ cats, but now has 50+ dogs. Costs of treatment, rehabilitation, and care are sustained solely through donations.

History

It started out as a halfway home for Ronald De Castro's rescued dog. It was originally known as Deparo Shelter, where Ronald rented a small place in Deparo.

Updates

SECASPI has been involved in spay and neuter, stray feeding programs, free vaccinations, and rescue missions. Some programs are sponsored by private partners.

Top 3 Reasons Why You Should Get Your Pet Neutered/Spayed

Our shelter supports getting your pet neutered/spayed. It is a scientific fact that spayed or neutered pets show improvement in their behavior. Spaying and neutering pets lower their chances of getting cancers, infections, and other health problems. The ultimate benefit of neutering and spaying is population control.

Our Services

Services That We Offer

Spay and Neuter

Our Spay and Neuter program is dedicated to controlling the pet population and improving animal health. By providing free spaying and neutering services, we help prevent unwanted litters, reduce the number of homeless animals, and promote longer, healthier lives for pets. Support our initiative to create a more sustainable and compassionate community for all animals.

Stray Animals Feeding Program

Our program aims to improve their quality of life and foster a healthier, more compassionate environment for all animals. Join us in our mission to care for these vulnerable creatures and make a tangible difference in their lives.

Free Vaccinations

Our Free Vaccinations program ensures that all animals at the shelter receive essential immunizations at no cost. By providing vital vaccines, we protect our pets from common diseases, promote their long-term health, and prepare them for adoption into loving homes. Join us in safeguarding the well-being of these animals and giving them a healthy start to their new lives.

Community Engagement

Our Community Engagement program aims to build strong relationships between the animal shelter and the local community. Through educational workshops, volunteer opportunities, and community events, we raise awareness about animal welfare and promote responsible pet ownership. By involving community members in our mission, we create a supportive network dedicated to improving the lives of animals.

Donation Drive

Our Donation Drive is crucial for supporting the needs of our animal shelter. By collecting funds, food, supplies, and other essentials, we ensure that every animal receives the care and attention they deserve. Your generous contributions help provide medical treatment, nutritious meals, and a safe, comfortable environment for our pets.
